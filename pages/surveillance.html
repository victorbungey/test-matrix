Surveillance

AI Powered CCTV Systems
Proactive security management

Details

To be efficiently proactive in management of security for our clients, we also offer Intelligent Video Surveillance (IVS) Solutions that make use of software that identify specific objects, behaviors/attitudes, faces, events, etc., in real-time. These systems are configured to classify events based on their criticality. When they detect events considered critical, they autonomously make decisions/take actions that would normally require human intervention, such as alerting operators, sending out alarms, shutting entrances, etc. We’ve partnered with leading firms, as implementation and development partners to scope, customize and implement AI-powered surveillance solutions for our clients.
