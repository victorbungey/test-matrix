Smart Key Management Systems
Keep an audit trail of all activities that involve taking & return of keys to the safe key cabinet.

Details

Facilities, staff, equipment and fleet security and safety depends largely on keys’ security and responsibility of their assignees. Our solution is a smart, secure and self-issuing key management system that uses Access Cards, PIN and Biometric Fingerprints to identify users and grant them access to fleet & premise keys that they are assigned to, and only during pre-set time periods. It also records the event and allows them to return the keys to secure slots within the stainless-steel key cabinet. The keys are identifiable by a RFID or iButton tag attached to them, to enable access to specific keys. Integratable with 3rd party security systems such as Access Control, CCTV, Alarm Systems, this ensures a holistic solution can be attained.
